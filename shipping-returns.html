Shipping & Returns

Find information regarding our shipping methods, delivery times, and returns policy.

1. Shipping Information

A. Processing Time:

Orders are typically processed within 1-3 business days (Monday-Friday, excluding holidays) after payment confirmation. Custom or made-to-order items may require additional processing time, which will be specified on the product page.

B. Shipping Methods & Costs:

We offer various shipping methods depending on your location and the size/weight of your order. Shipping costs will be calculated at checkout based on your delivery address and chosen shipping method. Options may include:

  • Standard Shipping (e.g., An Post, Royal Mail, DPD)
  • Express Shipping (available for select regions at an additional cost)

Estimated delivery times after dispatch:

  • Ireland: 2-5 business days
  • United Kingdom: 3-7 business days
  • Europe: 5-14 business days
  • International (Outside Europe): 7-21 business days (depending on destination and customs)

Please note that these are estimates and actual delivery times may vary due to unforeseen circumstances such as weather delays, customs processing, or peak seasons.

C. International Shipping & Customs:

For international orders, please be aware that customs duties, taxes, and fees may be imposed by the destination country's customs office. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in the item price or shipping cost. We recommend contacting your local customs office for more information.

D. Order Tracking:

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number, allowing you to monitor the progress of your delivery.

2. Returns & Exchanges

A. Our Policy:

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase from Arts & Lamps. If you are not entirely happy with your order, we accept returns and exchanges under the following conditions:

  • You must notify us of your intention to return or exchange an item within **14 days** of receiving your order.
  • The item must be returned in its original condition, unused, undamaged, and in its original packaging.
  • Custom or personalized items are generally not eligible for return or exchange unless they arrive damaged or defective.

B. How to Initiate a Return or Exchange:

To initiate a return or exchange, please contact us at [your contact email, e.g., anastasiafantasiaart@gmail.com] with your order number and the reason for the return/exchange. We will provide you with instructions on how to proceed.

C. Return Shipping Costs:

Unless the item is damaged or defective upon arrival, the customer is responsible for the cost of return shipping. We recommend using a trackable shipping service and purchasing shipping insurance, as we cannot guarantee that we will receive your returned item.

D. Refunds:

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email notification regarding the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within a certain number of days (e.g., 5-10 business days), depending on your bank or payment processor.

E. Damaged or Defective Items:

If your order arrives damaged or if you receive a defective item, please contact us immediately (within 48 hours of delivery) with clear photos of the damage and packaging. We will work with you to arrange a replacement or refund.

3. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Shipping & Returns policy, please do not hesitate to contact us at anastasiafantasiaart@gmail.com / thewedcrafterie@gmail.com / ivascaremus@gmail.com.
